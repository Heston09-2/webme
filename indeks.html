INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

IT (Information AND Technology), is an unofficial abbreviation/acronym in Indonesian. IT is a general term for technology that helps humans create, change, store, communicate and/or disseminate information.
Often hear the term IT (Information Technology) but still don't really understand what it means? IT is an abbreviation of Information Technology which, translated into Indonesian, means Information Technology. For a more in-depth explanation, keep reading this article until the end. In the modern era, the development of technology will be increasingly rapid, and this will make IT personnel increasingly needed. That is the reason why more and more universities are opening IT departments to continue producing these experts. The IT (Information Technology) major is one of the favorite majors because it is felt that it has a good future and is increasingly needed by many people. Therefore, it would be best for those of you who still don't understand IT, learn it now.
